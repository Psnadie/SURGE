— Questions

BEFORE YOU PADDLE OUT

Do I need my own board?

No. All classes include board rental as part of the fee. Our coaching team will select an appropriate shape and size for your level and local conditions on the day. If you have your own equipment and want to use it, let us know when booking.

What if I miss a session?

We understand that schedules change. Missed sessions can be rescheduled once per course block with 48 hours' notice. Sessions missed without notice are forfeited. We do not extend course timelines for multiple absences, but can arrange a private make-up session at an additional cost.

Are classes available year-round?

Yes. We run GROM and RIPPER sessions year-round from our North Shore base. CHARGER is seasonal — restricted to the October–April window when north swells are active and conditions match the advanced curriculum. Contact us for exact dates and availability.

What is the minimum age?

GROM classes accept students from age 10 upward. RIPPER requires a minimum age of 14. CHARGER is adults-only (18+) due to the advanced water conditions, tow-in equipment, and mental coaching components involved. All minors require a signed parental consent form prior to the first session.
