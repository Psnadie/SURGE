About Surge

SURGE was built for those who measure their sessions not in waves caught, but in walls confronted. We document the disciplines, the legends, and the athletes who push extreme surfing past every known limit.

From the Nazaré Canyon to Pe'ahi, from Pipeline barrels to Teahupo'o slabs — extreme surfing at its most raw, most consequential, most cinematic.
